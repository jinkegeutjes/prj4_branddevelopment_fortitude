About us

Get to know Fortitude!

  • Our Vision

    Fortitude strives to help every football player be the best version of themselves. Currently, 38% of the active football players experience active symptoms of depression and anxiety. We believe that is 38% too much. With the Fortitude app, we want to bring that percentage down to 0%.

  • Our Vision

    Calmth and Inner Peace

    Distress, discomposure and anxiety. These are emotions that we at Fortitude aim to eliminate within our users. Instead, we want you to feel like you are living as the best version of yourself, feeling a sense of calm and inner peace. We offer guided meditations to achieve this state of being.

  • Our Values

    Self-assurance

    Each game takes a lot of emotional and physical strength out of the player, football is a demanding sport. That is why Fortitude wants to assure that every player feels ultimately prepared before the game and walks onto the field feeling self-assured. Because only that way, they can give everything they have got. With our mindset execises you can make sure that walk onto the field will be your most comfortable

  • Our Values

    Confidence

    Every player deserves to feel like the best version of themselves. We want our users to feel confident both on and off the field so every game will become their best game yet. With Fortitude’s affirmations you can make sure to become the greatest version of yourself to-date!
